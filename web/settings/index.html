Account Data Deletion Instructions

At Pelegrin, we give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our servers, you can follow the instructions below.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Automated)

The fastest way to delete your data is directly from the Pelegrin app:

  1. Open the Pelegrin app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to your Profile tab on the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down and tap on Settings or the Delete Account option if visible.
  4. Confirm your decision. All your data will be immediately and permanently removed.

Option 2: Email Request (Manual)

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by contacting us:

  • Email us at: pelegrin@googlegroups.com
  • Subject Line: Account Deletion Request
  • In the email body, please provide the email address associated with your Pelegrin account so we can locate your data.
Important: Account deletion is permanent. Once completed, we will delete the following data associated with your account:
  • Your profile information (name, nationality, diocese, languages)
  • Your profile photo
  • All chat messages and match history
  • Your account credentials
This data cannot be recovered.
