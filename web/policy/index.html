Privacy Policy for Pelegrin

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Welcome to Pelegrin! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you create an account, such as:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your profile picture (optional)
  • Your "Diocese", location, or other match-making preferences
  • Chat messages sent to other users within the App

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Enable matchmaking and connection features for World Youth Day
  • Allow you to send messages to other users
  • Keep our community safe by monitoring reports of abuse

3. Sharing Your Information

Your profile information (name, photo, diocese, languages) is visible to other registered users of the App for matchmaking purposes. Your private messages are only visible to the recipient. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We use standard security measures, including Firebase Authentication and Firestore Security Rules, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Requesting Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the App's Profile settings or by contacting our support team at moveapp2@googlegroups.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at pelegrin@googlegroups.com.
